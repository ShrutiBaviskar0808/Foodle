# Product Overview

## Project Purpose
Foodle is a Flutter-based mobile application designed to help users track food preferences, allergies, and dietary needs of people in their social circle (family, friends, colleagues). The app enables better meal planning, party organization, and gift selection by maintaining comprehensive food preference profiles.

## Key Features
- **User Authentication System**: Complete signup/login flow with email verification and OTP-based security
- **Onboarding Experience**: Multi-screen introduction explaining app value proposition
- **Family Management**: Track food preferences for family members with personalized profiles
- **Favorite Places**: Maintain lists of preferred restaurants and dining locations
- **Friends Network**: Manage food preferences and dietary restrictions for friends
- **Cross-Platform Support**: Native implementations for Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, Linux, and Web

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Individuals who frequently plan meals, host gatherings, or organize social events
- **Secondary**: Family coordinators, event planners, and socially active individuals
- **Use Cases**: 
  - Planning family dinners with dietary restrictions in mind
  - Organizing parties and events with diverse food preferences
  - Selecting restaurants that accommodate everyone's needs
  - Gift planning involving food items

## Value Proposition
Eliminates the guesswork in food-related planning by centralizing preference data, reducing food waste, improving social dining experiences, and ensuring inclusive meal planning that considers everyone's dietary needs and restrictions.
